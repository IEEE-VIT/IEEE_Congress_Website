About IEEE and IEEE VIT SB

IEEE VIT SB is a student chapter under IEEE international and falls under Region 10 Asia Pacific, IEEE VIT SB is endowed with a stellar team of students, with the right blend of members who aim to foster technological innovation as a student organization at VIT. IEEE VIT (under Madras section) is one of the most active student branches of IEEE R10. Incorporating both technical and management brains, it’s the perfect blend for development of skills in both the fields. It is the most distinguished chapter of the Institution and has organized large scale events like Make-a-Thon,SPAx, NASA Space Apss, among many others.

TRACKS FOR TECHLOOP


Internet Of Things

The Internet of Things (IoT), the latest buzz in the Silicon Valley, is the interconnection of uniquely identifiable embedded computing devices within the existing Internet infrastructure. IoT is an emerging domain that has huge applications in different arenas of the computer industry. IoT promotes a heightened level of awareness about our world, and a platform from which to monitor the reactions to the changing conditions that said awareness exposes us to.


Android App Development

App development means the core development of software particularly for the smart phones and other gadgets. The android is a powerful operating system and it supports large number of applications in Smartphones today. In the A+ track of techloop, the students will be taught how to develop and build different applications of Android, which is an important skill in the domain of app development today.


Machine Learning

Machine learning is the science of getting computers to act without being explicitly programmed. In the past decade, machine learning has given us self-driving cars, practical speech recognition, effective web search, and a vastly improved understanding of the human genome. With the constant evolution of the field, there has been a subsequent rise in the uses, demands, and importance of machine learning.
